Robotics Lab Kiosk Terms and Conditions

By using this kiosk to register, check in, or confirm your identity for lab access, you acknowledge the following:

1. Information Collected
The kiosk may collect and store the information you enter into the system, including your name, student ID, phone number, myMDC username, and school email address.
When an ID card is used, the kiosk may also process card-derived information needed to identify the cardholder, support check-in, and link a card to the correct student record.
The system may also create and store daily check-in records, signed behavioral contract PDFs, and photos taken during registration or check-in.

2. Purpose
This information is used for student registration, lab check-in, identity handling, safety, equipment-space accountability, and normal club/lab recordkeeping.

3. Conduct Expectations
Users are expected to follow lab rules, respect staff and other students, use equipment only as authorized, and comply with supervision and access requirements for the space.
Violations may result in removal from the space or suspension of lab privileges, as determined by staff.

4. Acknowledgment
By using this kiosk, you confirm that the information you provide is accurate and that you understand the lab may keep the records described above as part of normal operations.
